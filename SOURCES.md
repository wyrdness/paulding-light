# Sources & References

## Scientific Studies

### Primary Research
- Michigan Technological University students (2010). *Investigation of the Paulding Light*. Conclusive study demonstrating car headlight origin through synchronized observation.
  - Methodology: Coordinated observation with Highway 45 traffic
  - Findings: Perfect correlation between vehicle movements and light appearances
  - Conclusion: Atmospheric refraction of car headlights
